# Cinders

**A grim, staccato study in industrial dread**

---

## Overview

"Cinders" reimagines Moderat's "Reminder" through a dark, fragmented lens - staccato pulses, distorted bass, harsh textures, and a sense of collapse. The title evokes burning bridges reduced to ash, the aftermath of fire, and the industrial decay of what remains.
