# Sun Punch

**A staccato Afrobeat-Haitian fusion study at 90 BPM**

---

## Overview

"Sun Punch" captures the uplifting warmth of Caribbean-Afrobeat fusion with a percussive, staccato bite. At 90 BPM, the track balances relaxed island vibes with punchy rhythmic attacks - sunshine with a sharp edge.

**Inspired by:** Wesli's "Ayayay"

---

**To make it more punchy:**
```ruby

```

**To make it more relaxed:**
```ruby
# Increase sustain slightly
```
