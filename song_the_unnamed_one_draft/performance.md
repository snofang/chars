Here's a performance guide for 4/4 time:

---

**4/4 TIME PERFORMANCE GUIDE**

**THE PULSE**
Four beats per measure. Quarter note gets the beat.
Count: **1** - 2 - 3 - 4 | **1** - 2 - 3 - 4
The downbeat (1) is home. The others lead back.

**WEIGHT**
- Beat 1: Heaviest — the anchor
- Beat 3: Secondary weight — the turn
- Beats 2 & 4: Light — the breath between

**FEEL**
- **Rock** (backbeat on 2 & 4) → drive forward
- **Straight** (equal weight) → march, steady
- **Swing** (triplet feel) → roll, glide

**PHRASING**
Most phrases land in 4, 8, or 16 bars.
Listen for the turnaround at bar 8.
The resolution comes at bar 16.

**DYNAMICS**
- Build tension from 1 toward 4
- Release on the next 1
- Let the measure breathe as a full cycle

**COMMON PATTERNS**
- Kick on 1 & 3, Snare on 2 & 4
- Hi-hat on eighth notes for flow
- Bass moves on 1, chord changes on 3

**FOR PERFORMANCE**
- Lock the downbeat
- Let the offbeats move
- When in doubt, feel 1
- When lost, count 1-2-3-4 aloud until found

---

That's it. The rest is listening.
