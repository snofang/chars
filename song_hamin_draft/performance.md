**Performance Script — «همین» (with Bang)**  
*(Andante calmo → Explosive → Fragile Return)*  

---

**[Intro — 4 bars, piano only]**  
Left hand: soft, repeated Dm–Am–C arpeggios  
Right hand: single-note melody, simple and exposed  
*Dynamics: p, semplice, con pedale leggero*  

---

**[Verse 1 — 4 bars]**  
*mp, parlante (spoken-sung)*  

همین که می‌فهمی، *(breath)*  
کاری از دستت برآمده. *(breath)*  
درد را بگذار *(breath)*  
برای جایی که برآید. *(breath, hold)*  

---

**[Chorus 1 — 8 bars]**  
*mf, più caldo*  

درد که می‌آید، نگاهش کن— *(breath)*  
اما نرو از خودت. *(breath)*  
بگذار ببیند که کسی هست *(breath)*  
که دیدنش را تاب می‌آوری. *(breath, hold 2 beats)*  

*(Repeat Chorus 1 with slight crescendo on "ببیند" and decay on "تاب می‌آوری")*  

---

**[Verse 2 — 4 bars]**  
*mp, più intimo*  

نگاه اول، خود را می‌بیند. *(breath)*  
نگاه دوم، خود را با خود می‌برد. *(breath)*  
تسلیت نمی‌گوید «حالا چه»— *(breath)*  
می‌گوید «با خودت هستی، همین.» *(breath, hold)*  

---

**[Chorus 2 — 8 bars]**  
*mf, espressivo*  

درد که می‌آید، نگاهش کن— *(breath)*  
اما نرو از خودت. *(breath)*  
بگذار ببیند که کسی هست *(breath)*  
که دیدنش را تاب می‌آوری. *(breath, hold 2 beats)*  

*(Repeat Chorus 2 with a gentle swell on "کسی هست" and a soft decay on "تاب می‌آوری")*  

---

**[Bridge — 4 bars, gathering]**  
*crescendo poco a poco, mp → f*  

رفتی از خودت—چه دیدی؟ *(breath, intensity building)*  
ماندی در خودت—چه غمی می‌ماند؟ *(breath, hold, f)*  

---

**[Bang — 1 bar, fff, explosive]**  
*Full force: piano crash (D minor cluster), low drum hit, cymbal swell*  
*All voices: one sustained, unleashed cry—breaking through the stillness*  

---

**[Silence — 2 bars]**  
*Absolute silence. Let it ring. Let it settle.*  

---

**[Outro — 6 bars, after the silence]**  
*ppp, come da lontano (from far away), fragile, almost a whisper*  

همین که می‌فهمی، *(breath, barely there)*  
کاری از دستت برآمده. *(breath, fragile)*  
درد را بگذار *(breath, a thread of sound)*  
برای جایی که برآید. *(breath, fermata, fade into absolute silence)*  

---

**Final Dynamics Map:**  

| Section | Dynamics | Feel |
|---------|----------|------|
| Intro | p | open, spacious |
| Verse 1 | mp | calm, clear |
| Chorus 1 | mf | warm, inviting |
| Verse 2 | mp | intimate, inward |
| Chorus 2 | mf → f | fuller, more present |
| Bridge | mp → f | gathering weight |
| **Bang!** | **fff** | **explosive, shattering** |
| Silence | — | let it breathe |
| Outro | ppp → niente | fragile, returning, dissolving |

---

**Bang Notation Details — «همین»:**  
— **Piano:** cluster chord (D, Eb, F, G, Ab, B, D) slammed with the forearm, pedal held through the decay  
— **Drums:** single, massive bass drum hit + cymbal crash, letting it ring  
— **Strings:** all instruments hit a low, sustained D minor chord, then release into silence  
— **Voice:** one raw, primal cry—no words, just sound, held for as long as breath allows  
— **After the bang:** absolute silence for 2 full bars—let the weight settle before the outro begins  

---

**Breath Mark Legend:**  
— *(breath)* = natural pause, about one beat  
— *(hold)* = hold 2–3 beats  
— *(hold 2 beats)* = longer pause, letting the silence speak  
— *(fermata)* = hold as long as feels right, then release into silence  

---

**Instrumentation Notes for the Bang:**  
— The bang is the moment the question "چه دیدی؟" and "چه غمی می‌ماند؟" answers itself  
— After the bang, the silence is not empty—it is the space where understanding settles  
— The outro returns to the opening words, but now they mean something else:  
  *Not "I understand, so I can act," but "I understand, so I can stay."*  

---

**The Meaning Behind the Bang:**  
The bang is not destruction.  
It is the moment you stop running from yourself—  
and realize you were never lost.  
The silence after is not absence.  
It is presence, finally still.
