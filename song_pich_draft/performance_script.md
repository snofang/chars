**Performance Script — «پیچ»**  
*(Adagio sostenuto, ≈ 68 bpm, E minor)*  

---

**[Intro — 4 bars, piano only]**  
Left hand: deep, wide arpeggios (Em–G–Bm)  
Right hand: sparse, resonant single notes, as if echoing from a distance  
*Dynamics: p, molto legato, con pedale*  

---

**[Verse 1 — 4 bars]**  
*mp, calmo, narrativo*  

تا وقتی گرمیِ آغوشِ خاطره هست، *(breath)*  
آغوش دیگری جایش نمی‌ایستد— *(breath, hold)*  

---

**[Verse 2 — 4 bars]**  
*mp, stesso tempo*  

تا وقتی طنینِ گام‌هایی که می‌شناختی، *(breath)*  
گامِ تازه‌ای هماهنگ‌اش نمی‌پیچد— *(breath, hold)*  

---

**[Verse 3 — 4 bars]**  
*mp, più pesante (heavier), grounded*  

تا وقتی نقشِ دستی که روزی گرفتت، *(breath)*  
دستِ دگر همان‌گونه نمی‌فشرد— *(breath, hold)*  

---

**[Verse 4 — 4 bars]**  
*mp, più leggero (lighter), more open*  

تا وقتی تابشِ نورِ آن پنجره‌ی دور، *(breath)*  
چراغی دیگر آن سایه نمی‌اندازد— *(breath, hold)*  

---

**[Verse 5 — 4 bars]**  
*mp, sospeso (suspended), breathy*  

تا وقتی عطرِ بارانِ شبِ آشنایی، *(breath)*  
زمینِ تازه‌شسته بویش را نمی‌آرد— *(breath, hold)*  

---

**[Verse 6 — 4 bars]**  
*mp, intimo, inward*  

تا وقتی آهنگِ نفسی که کنارت بود، *(breath)*  
نَفَسی دیگر آن حال و هوا را ندارد— *(breath, hold 2 beats)*  

---

**[Outro — 8 bars]**  
*mf, espressivo, gathering gently*  

ما خود را به خویشتن باختیم، *(breath)*  
در پیچی که هم شروع است و هم پایان، *(breath)*  
و هر بار که بازمی‌گردیم به خود، *(breath)*  
همان را می‌یابیم که گم کرده بودیم. *(breath, fermata on last note, fade to niente)*  

---

**Final Dynamics Map:**  

| Section | Dynamics | Feel |
|---------|----------|------|
| Intro | p | distant, resonant |
| Verses 1–6 | mp | steady, unhurried, each verse like a turn of the wheel |
| Outro | mf → p → pp | gathering, then releasing; circular, no final resolution |
| Final note | pp → niente | dissolve into silence, as if the wheel keeps turning beyond hearing |

---

**Breath Mark Legend:**  
— *(breath)* = a natural pause, about one beat, to let the line settle  
— *(hold)* = hold the note for 2–3 beats before continuing  
— *(hold 2 beats)* = a longer pause, letting the silence speak  
— *(fermata)* = hold as long as feels right, then release into silence  

---

**Repeat Structure:**  
— Verses 1–6: sung once through, with a slight dynamic lift on each repeated phrase  
— Outro: one pass only, decrescendo to niente  
— No chorus—each verse is its own turn, building toward the final gathering  

---

**Instrumentation Notes:**  
— Piano: left hand arpeggios throughout, deep and wide; right hand melody, simple and resonant  
— Optional: cello or bass clarinet on the Outro, holding long, dark tones, echoing the final lines  
— Silence between verses is essential—each line needs room to land before the next begins  
— The piece should feel like a slow rotation, returning to itself, never quite resolving
