**Performance Script — «پیچ» (with Bang)**  
*(Adagio sostenuto, ≈ 68 bpm, E minor → E major)*  

---

**[Intro — 4 bars, piano only]**  
Left hand: deep, wide arpeggios (Em–G–Bm)  
Right hand: sparse, resonant single notes, as if echoing from a distance  
*Dynamics: p, molto legato, con pedale*  

---

**[Verses 1–6 — 4 bars each, pp → mp]**  
*(Each verse builds imperceptibly, like a slow wave)*  

تا وقتی گرمیِ آغوشِ خاطره هست، *(breath)*  
آغوش دیگری جایش نمی‌ایستد— *(breath, hold)*  

تا وقتی طنینِ گام‌هایی که می‌شناختی، *(breath)*  
گامِ تازه‌ای هماهنگ‌اش نمی‌پیچد— *(breath, hold)*  

تا وقتی نقشِ دستی که روزی گرفتت، *(breath)*  
دستِ دگر همان‌گونه نمی‌فشرد— *(breath, hold)*  

تا وقتی تابشِ نورِ آن پنجره‌ی دور، *(breath)*  
چراغی دیگر آن سایه نمی‌اندازد— *(breath, hold)*  

تا وقتی عطرِ بارانِ شبِ آشنایی، *(breath)*  
زمینِ تازه‌شسته بویش را نمی‌آرد— *(breath, hold)*  

تا وقتی آهنگِ نفسی که کنارت بود، *(breath)*  
نَفَسی دیگر آن حال و هوا را ندارد— *(breath, hold 2 beats)*  

---

**[Bridge — 2 bars, crescendo]**  
*crescendo subito, from mp → fff*  

همین— *(short, sharp breath)*  
همین— *(bang! full force)*  

---

**[Bang — 1 bar, fff, explosive]**  
*Full ensemble: piano crash (E minor cluster), low drum hit, cymbal swell*  
*All voices: one sustained, unleashed note—raw, unpolished, breaking through the silence*  

---

**[Outro — 8 bars, after the bang]**  
*Suddenly silent. Then, from the rubble:*  
*ppp, come da lontano (from far away), almost like a whisper*  

ما خود را به خویشتن باختیم، *(breath, fragile)*  
در پیچی که هم شروع است و هم پایان، *(breath, barely there)*  
و هر بار که بازمی‌گردیم به خود، *(breath, a thread of sound)*  
همان را می‌یابیم که گم کرده بودیم. *(breath, fermata on last note, fade into absolute silence)*  

---

**Final Dynamics Map:**  

| Section | Dynamics | Feel |
|---------|----------|------|
| Intro | p | distant, resonant |
| Verses 1–6 | pp → mp | slow, inevitable build |
| Bridge | mp → fff | sudden gathering of all weight |
| **Bang!** | **fff** | **explosive, shattering** |
| Outro | ppp → niente | fragile, surviving, returning to the center |

---

**Bang Notation Details:**  
— **Piano:** cluster chord (E, F, G, B, C, E) slammed with the forearm, pedal held through the decay  
— **Drums:** single, massive bass drum hit + cymbal crash, letting it ring  
— **Strings:** all instruments hit a low, sustained E minor chord, then release into silence  
— **Voice:** one raw, primal cry—no words, just sound, held for as long as breath allows  

---

**Breath Mark Legend:**  
— *(breath)* = natural pause, about one beat  
— *(hold)* = hold 2–3 beats  
— *(short, sharp breath)* = quick gasp before the bang  
— *(fermata)* = hold until silence takes over  

---

**Instrumentation Notes for the Bang:**  
— The bang should feel like the center of the piece—the moment the turning breaks  
— After the bang, the silence is the most important part—let it sit before the outro begins  
— The outro should feel like coming back to yourself after losing everything—fragile, but still there  

---

**The Meaning Behind the Bang:**  
The bang is not destruction.  
It is the moment the wheel stops—  
and you realize you were never turning away from yourself.  
The silence after is not emptiness.  
It is return.
