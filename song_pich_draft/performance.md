**Performance Notes**  
Tempo: Adagio sostenuto (≈ 68 bpm)  
Time Signature: 4/4  
Key: E minor (relative G major)  
Dynamics: piano throughout, with slight swells on repeated phrases (sempre piano, ma espressivo)

**Repeat Structure:**  
Verses 1–6: sung once through, then repeated with slight dynamic lift (mp on second pass)  
Outro: one pass only, decrescendo to niente  

**Instrumentation suggestion:**  
— Piano left hand: soft, wide arpeggios (E minor–G major–B minor)  
— Right hand: sparse, single-note melody, echoing the Farsi phrasing
